Turning OKRs into Actionable Portfolio Decisions

Published September 11, 2025

OKRs are inspiring, but on their own they don’t drive change. The bridge is a portfolio process that links each initiative directly to a key result and to real team capacity.

Make OKRs actionable

  1. Anchor every initiative to a key result.
  2. Frame problems before solutions.
  3. Prioritize ruthlessly using impact, risk, and alignment.
  4. Check team readiness and load.

With this connection, work reflects strategy and leaders see overload, risk, and progress at a glance.
