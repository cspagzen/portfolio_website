How I Work

Over 20 years, I've built products and teams across Fortune 500 enterprises, hypergrowth startups, and everything in between. I've learned that great product leadership isn't about applying frameworks. It's about understanding what actually moves the business, making hard tradeoffs, and building teams that execute. I focus on clarity over complexity, strategic focus over activity, and outcomes over outputs. Whether scaling from startup to growth stage or transforming established organizations, I help companies figure out what actually matters and how to ship it.
